Privacy Policy

Triple Treat

Effective date: 2026-02-09

This Privacy Policy explains how Triple Treat ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the Triple Treat mobile application and related services (the "Service").

Triple Treat Project Team (based in Sweden) acts as the data controller for personal data processed through the Service.

1. Purpose of the Service

Triple Treat is a kitchen and restaurant management application that allows users to manage orders, menus, inventory, staff access, and analytics through role-based kitchen access.

2. Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data

  • Email address (authentication, verification, password reset).
  • Display name (if provided).
  • Account metadata (creation date, last sign-in timestamp, verification status).

2.2 Kitchen and Operational Data

  • Kitchens created or joined (kitchen ID, name, owner ID, timestamps).
  • Membership roles and approval status.
  • Orders, menu items, inventory records, and related timestamps.
  • Kitchen settings (currency, categories, business-day configuration).

2.3 Technical Information

We may receive limited technical information necessary to operate and secure the Service, such as app version, device type, timestamps, and error logs. We do not intentionally collect precise location data.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Performance of a contract: To provide account authentication and core features.
  • Legitimate interests: To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve functionality.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by applicable law.
  • Consent: For optional features where consent is requested.

4. How We Use Personal Data

  • Provide authentication and account management.
  • Enable kitchen collaboration and role-based access.
  • Maintain system integrity and prevent misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We share data only as necessary to operate the Service:

  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase (Authentication and Database services) to host and process data. Firebase acts as a data processor under its applicable terms and data processing agreements.
  • Kitchen Members: Data entered into a kitchen is visible to other approved members according to their role.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights and security.

6. International Transfers

Data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as your account remains active or as necessary to provide the Service. When you delete your account in-app, personal account data is removed, subject to technical limitations and legal retention requirements.

8. Your Rights (EEA/UK/Switzerland)

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Object to certain processing activities.
  • Request data portability.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

In Sweden, the supervisory authority is Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten (IMY).

9. Account Deletion and Data Export

  • You may delete your account directly within the app.
  • You may export your account-related data in JSON format before deletion.
  • If you are the sole owner of a kitchen, deletion may also remove associated kitchen data.

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. However, no system is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

11. Children

The Service is intended for business use and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the Service after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
